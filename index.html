nowt interesting

(na ät) - nowt is sometimes used to mean the same as 'nothing'

the concept of this personal project came about during 2020's summer lockdown
i have always been fascinated with the storytelling behind photography
whether that be of people, places, or both
this fascination resulted in me buying my first ever camera, an original fuji x100
to which people often questioned: "what are you gna take pictures of?"
of which my reply would always be: "nowt interesting"

everything you see here is entirely authentic, done & designed by myself
from the photos to the html, css & javascript powering this website

a playground for creativity through photos, art & code
