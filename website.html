about me . . .

my name is daniel taylor, a data engineer at centrica plc.
i have decided to produce this website as a hobby, to showcase some of the photos i take with my fuji x100, whilst also building some html, css & js skills.
most photos will be taken in either london, my home of work, or sheffield, where i grew up.
the theme of all photos is nowt interesting, boring, everyday stuff.
this will be used as a playground project, maybe expanding out into a blog or personal brand.
